Reflections
gasp

I have witnessed teenage geniuses on this campus and have welcomed celebrities who have returned to campus with honor. Those moments are wonderful, but I know that they don’t always come to all of us. Not everything we do is rewarded, and not everything we aspire to is finally attainable. Most of us, for the most part, are quietly ploughing our way to our ideals and what we love. What is even more regrettable is that in our lives and academic lives, there will be countless injustices and helpless bumps in the road.

Most things in the world don’t make much sense.
Except for truth and love.
